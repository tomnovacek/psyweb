# About Me

I am a licensed psychotherapist with over 10 years of experience in the field. My passion for helping people and deep understanding of human psychology drives me to provide the best possible care to each client.

## My Approach

I believe in a holistic approach to mental health that considers the whole person - body, mind, and spirit. My therapeutic style is:

- **Empathetic:** I create a safe and supportive space for your healing
- **Client-centered:** I tailor therapy to your specific needs
- **Evidence-based:** I use proven therapeutic methods
- **Culturally sensitive:** I respect and value your cultural background

## Education and Qualifications

- Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology, Charles University
- M.Sc. in Psychology, Charles University
- Certified Cognitive Behavioral Therapist
- Certified EMDR Therapist
- Member of the Czech Psychotherapeutic Society

## Professional Experience

- Private Practice (2018 - present)
- Clinical Psychologist, Psychiatric Clinic (2015 - 2018)
- Research Assistant, Institute of Psychology (2013 - 2015)

## Specializations

- Anxiety Disorders
- Depression
- Trauma and PTSD
- Relationship Issues
- Personal Growth and Development
- Intercultural Psychology

## My Philosophy

I believe that every person has the capacity to grow and heal within themselves. My role as a therapist is to be your guide on this journey, providing support and tools needed for positive change.

## Outside the Office

When I'm not in the office, I engage in:

- Research in psychotherapy
- Writing professional articles
- Conducting workshops and seminars
- Traveling and exploring different cultures
- Meditation and mindfulness practice
