## Initial Consultation

The initial consultation lasts 30 minutes and is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. During this session, we:

- Discuss your concerns and goals
- I introduce you to my therapeutic approach
- Answer your questions
- Decide together on the next steps

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long is a therapy session?
Standard sessions are 50 minutes for individual therapy and 60 minutes for couples therapy.

### How often do sessions occur?
Session frequency is individual and depends on your needs. We typically meet once a week.

### Is therapy covered by insurance?
I work with most major insurance providers. Please contact me for more information about insurance coverage options.

### Do you offer online therapy?
Yes, I offer online therapy sessions for your convenience and flexibility.
